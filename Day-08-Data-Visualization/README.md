# Day 08 – Data Visualization in Excel

## What is Data Visualization?

Data Visualization is the graphical representation of data using charts and graphs.  
It helps analysts understand patterns, trends, and insights quickly.

---

## Types of Charts

### 1. Bar Chart
Used to compare different categories.

Example:
Sales by product category.

### 2. Pie Chart
Used to show percentage contribution.

Example:
Revenue share by category.

### 3. Line Chart
Used to show trends over time.

Example:
Monthly sales growth.

---

## Why Visualization is Important

Businesses prefer visual reports because they make complex data easy to understand and help in decision making.
